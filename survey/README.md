# survey/ — the srsRAN restructuring lens

A curated literature corpus underpins this study (AI-RAN, RAN optimization,
AI-RAN orchestration, heterogeneous scheduling). It is maintained privately and
shared with collaborators on request — not duplicated in this public repo.

This folder holds the *lens* over that corpus specific to this study: what the
papers actually say about srsRAN, CU/DU restructuring, scheduler efficiency, and
AI-RAN — and the gaps they leave for us.

Empty until the lens is written.
